Grand Opening!

As of September 2016, The Gymrek Lab has opened its doors!

We are located in two departments. In the Department of Medicine, we are in the first floor of the CNCB building in shared wet lab space with Alon Goren's lab. We have additional space in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering close to other bioinformatics groups.

We are looking for motivated and talented members to join our team on this exciting journey. To learn more, see the jobs page.

Stay tuned to see what we're up to!
